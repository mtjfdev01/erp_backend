=====================================
  CRON JOB TIMINGS  (Asia/Karachi)
=====================================

DAILY
-----
  01:00 AM  — Donations Daily Report          (donations-report-cron.service.ts)
  01:30 AM  — Meezan Donation Status Sync     (dms_crons/dms-crons.service.ts)
  02:30 AM  — Dashboard Rebuild Nightly       (dashboard/dashboard-rebuild-cron.service.ts)
  09:00 AM  — Gold & Silver Price Fetch       (zakat/gold_silver_price/gold-silver-price-cron.service.ts)

WEEKLY
------
  Monday 09:00 AM  — Donations Weekly Report  (donations-report-cron.service.ts)

MONTHLY
-------
  1st of month 09:00 AM  — Donations Monthly Report  (donations-report-cron.service.ts)
  2nd of month 09:00 AM  — Manual Recurring Donation Reminders  (dms_crons/dms-crons.service.ts)
    • Processes pledges in chunks (default 500, env MANUAL_RECURRING_CHUNK_SIZE)
    • Optional pause between chunks: MANUAL_RECURRING_CHUNK_DELAY_MS (default 250ms)

MANUAL (API endpoints)
----------------------
  POST /dms-crons/sync-meezan       — One-time Meezan status sync   (dms_crons/dms-crons.controller.ts)
  POST /dms-crons/manual-recurring-reminders — Manual recurring reminder job (dms_crons/dms-crons.controller.ts)
  POST /donations-report/daily       — Manual daily report trigger   (donations_report/donations-report.controller.ts)
  POST /donations-report/weekly      — Manual weekly report trigger  (donations_report/donations-report.controller.ts)
  POST /donations-report/monthly     — Manual monthly report trigger (donations_report/donations-report.controller.ts)
